Drew's 2007 top 6

Accompanying Image: 
smd_2007comp_2.jpg

Drew Brockington (aka capt.geech) has given us this telling list of 6 of his many favorite tracks from 2007.

  1. What I Love the Most - Beat Radio (from The Great Big Sea and Miracle Flag EP)
  2. Jesu Mix - Explosions in the Sky (from All of a Sudden I Miss Everyone )
  3. The Temptation of Adam - Josh Ritter (from The Historical Conquests of Josh Ritter)
  4. Lay Your Head Down - Keren Ann (from Keren Ann)
  5. Fake Empire - The National (from Boxer)
  6. Time Left For Love - Shout Out Louds (from Our Ill Wills)
