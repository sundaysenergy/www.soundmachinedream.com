And Now For Something We Hope You Really Like!



It's becoming fall, which means that the stores are starting to put their pumpkin ales back on the shelf. Last year I took a few months to sample as many ales as possible from this specific category. This year, I am carrying on the tradition.



The first pick is the DOGFISH HEAD PUNKIN ALE. It has a strong tinny first taste, with a nice pumpkin pie finnish.



Generally I like the pumpkin to hit you first, as a refreshing little blast, and then finish with all the nice ol' tangy goodness that ales provide. It was ok, a nice first of the season.



NOW SOMETHING MUSIC RELATED ON THIS SUBJECT:



A GREAT SONG TO ENJOY PUMPKIN ALE TO:



Strawberry Hill, by Coldplay on their new album VIVA LA VIDA. O've been enjoying that album for a few days, it's growing on me.
